== Description ==

### WP Stripe Cart: Manage your products on Stripe and sell in your site.

If you want to sell only a few products, setting up woo-commerce, or the other ecommerce plugins are too much to do.
And using third party ASP cart services is not a option either, then WP Stripe Cart is your choise!

Manage you products on Stripe, then use Stripe products number to sell your products in your web site.

Only thing you have to do is, to insert this shortcode to you posts, or pages.
WP Stripe Cart will take care the rest.

`[wsc-get-product product_id=stripe_products_id_here]`

== Screenshots ==

1. Register your product to Stripe. Then copy product_id
2. Insert your product_id with provided shortcode. Product section will appear in your post or page.
3. Cart icon will be shown on right bottom of screen.
4. Shopping cart screen. Mobile first and responsive layout.
